# Help

## Having trouble?

If you encounter any technical issues during the study, try the following:

1. **Refresh the page** — Your progress is saved automatically.
2. **Use a desktop browser** — This study works best on Chrome, Firefox, or Edge on a desktop or laptop computer.
3. **Ensure images load** — If charts are not displaying, check your internet connection and try refreshing.

## Study Questions

- **What am I supposed to do?** On each trial, compare the charted asset (Option A) to the fixed-odds investment (Option B) and choose the one you'd prefer.
- **Is there a right answer?** There are no trick questions. Use your best judgment to pick the investment you think is better.
- **How long will this take?** Approximately 15–20 minutes.
